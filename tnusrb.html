Tamil Nadu Uniformed Services Recruitment Board Sub Inspector (TNUSRB SI)

Qualification: UG in any Degree

Age:

  • Candidates between 20 and 28 years of age are eligible to apply for the post.
  • Upper age limit for BC, BC (M), MBC/ DNC (De-notified Community) is up to 30 years
  • Upper age limit for SC, SC(A), ST is up to 33 years
  • Upper age limit for Ex-servicemen/Ex-personnel of Central Para-military Forces is up to 45 years
  • Upper age limit for Departmental candidates appearing for departmental quota examination is up to 45 years

Pattern of the exam:

  • Written Examination: For open quota candidates, objective type questions (MCQs) of 70 marks are being asked as per TNUSRB SI syllabus, while for departmental quota candidates, objective type questions (MCQs) of 85 marks are being asked.
  • Physical Measurement Test (PMT) / Endurance Test (ET) / Physical Efficiency test (PET): PMT and ET are of qualifying nature. PET will contain maximum of 15 marks. Departmental candidates donâ€™t need to appear for PMT and PET. Endurance Test will be qualifying in nature. Ex-servicemen, EX-CPMF and Serving persons, who will be retiring within a year, are also exempted from PMT.
  • Viva-voce/Interview: This is face-to-face round, maximum marks allotted is 10 for open and departmental quota candidates, both.
  • Special Marks: Candidates, in both the category, holding special NCC/NSS/Sports certificates will get extra 5 marks.

Tamil Nadu Uniformed Services Recruitment Board Sub Inspector (TNUSRB SI)

Posts:

  • GR. II Police Constables
  • Gr. II Jail Warders
  • Firemen

Qualification:

  • Candidate should be 10th class passed.
  • Tamil language as one of the subjects in the 10th standard.

Age:

Categories Age Limit
For General 18 years to 24 years
For MBCs/DCs, BCs 18 years to 26 years
For SCs, SC(A)s, STs 18 years to 29 years
For Transgender 18 years to 29 years
For Female Destitute Widows 18 years to 35 years
For Ex-Serviceman 18 years to 45 years

Pattern of the exam:

  • Written Examination: An objective-type, offline exam of 80 marks.
  • Physical Measurement Test (PMT) / Endurance Test (ET) / Physical Efficiency test (PET): it will be a total of 15 marks.
  • Special Marks: Extra 5 marks will be given to the candidates having NCC/NSS/sports certificates.
  • Note: The total of all the stages will be 100 marks. The final merit list will be made considering the performance in all the stages of the exam. Selected candidates will be called in for Document verification. The candidates are required to provide original certificates and/or documents for verification. Any discrepancy in the verification in the documents will result in disqualification from the examination. After the verification, a list of selected candidates will be posted.
