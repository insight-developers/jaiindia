Pattern of the exam:
TNPSC Group 1 exam takes place in three stages:

  • Prelims – one objective paper – 300 marks
  • Mains – three descriptive papers – 750 marks total
  • Interview – 100 marks

Eligibility Criteria:

Educational Qualification: UG in any Degree
